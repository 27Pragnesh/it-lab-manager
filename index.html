QR Code Generator
Generate and print QR codes to stick on each PC
🔗 How QR Codes Work Each QR code encodes a unique URL for that PC, e.g. https://yourlab.web.app/checkin?lab=A&pc=PC-01
When a student scans it, their phone opens the check-in form pre-filled with the lab and PC number. Set your deployed app URL below before generating.

Print tip
1. Click Print All QR Codes
2. Set paper size to A4, print 6 per page
3. Cut each QR card and laminate it
4. Stick on top of the PC monitor or keyboard

Recommended size: 8cm × 8cm per label
Include: PC number and lab name on the label
